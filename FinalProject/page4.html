Pro League

Many Video games with a large player bases end up with a pro league of the best players from across the world. The E Sports Pro League is just like any other professional sports league with two teams facing off against each with the winner taking a reward of money, up to 100s of thousands of dollars. There are even commentators who watch the match and describe whats happening, these tend to be veteran players who have played the game for a while but usually not as good as the pro players.

Fun Fact

Within the Pro league Team Penta is widely regarded a the best team with them winning 2 of the 3 three world championships.
