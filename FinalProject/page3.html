Attacking

The second game mode is defending. When a match is started you will be placed on either blue or orange team. Orange team always starts on defence. After the match starts you will be put in a screen where you can choose one of the 17 defending operators. On defence your objective is to either eliminate all attacking players or to complete the major objective i.e. defending a hostage or protecting a bomb.
