Fun Fact

Currently there are 33 total characters with 16 on offense and 17 on defence.

Attacking

The first game mode is attacking. When a match is started you will be placed on either blue or orange team. blue team always starts on offence. After the match starts you will be put in a screen where you can choose one of the 16 attacking operators, but only one of each operator is permitted on you team. On offence your objective is to either eliminate all defending players or to complete the major objective i.e. rescuing a hostage or defusing a bomb.
